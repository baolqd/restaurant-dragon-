About us

Welcome To Dragon Restaurant

Dragon Restaurant is proud to introduce a diverse VietNamese Cuisine with many dishes with unique flavors from our homeland. That distinct flavor is created by the hands of talented and experienced chefs from many parts of VietNam that we are fortunate to cooperate with, promising to make every diners who come to our Restaurant leave with complete satisfaction.

Dragon Restaurant is a close rendezvous for international friends to come and enjoy many typical dishes from North to South along this 'S'-shaped land. Or if you are VietNamese , you should also come to try and experience national dishes in a new way.

Dragon Restaurant bring dishes with careful and enthusiastic preparation, from the selection of ingredients and spices to the decoration of the dishes, with the purpose of bringing the most joyful to our dinners.
